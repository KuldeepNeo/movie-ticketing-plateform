# 1. Vision Statement

Build a modern, scalable, and intuitive movie ticket booking platform that enables users to discover movies playing in their city, select preferred theaters and seats, complete bookings seamlessly, and access digital tickets from anywhere.

The platform aims to provide a frictionless end-to-end booking experience comparable to leading movie ticketing platforms while maintaining simplicity, reliability, and excellent user experience.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Traditional ticket booking often involves:

* Long queues at theaters
* Uncertainty about seat availability
* Limited information about movies
* Poor booking experience
* No centralized booking history
* Difficulty managing tickets

Users need a digital solution that allows them to book tickets quickly and securely from any device.

---

# 3. Product Vision

The Movie Ticketing Platform will become a centralized destination where users can:

* Discover movies currently playing in their city
* Explore movie details before making a decision
* Compare theaters and showtimes
* Select seats interactively
* Complete bookings within minutes
* Access digital tickets anytime
* Manage previous bookings from a single account

The platform prioritizes speed, usability, accuracy, and reliability throughout the booking journey.

---

# 4. Product Goals

## Primary Goals

* Deliver a seamless movie booking experience.
* Minimize the number of steps required to complete a booking.
* Prevent seat conflicts and double-booking.
* Provide real-time seat availability.
* Offer a responsive experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
* Maintain a secure authentication and booking process.

---

## Secondary Goals

* Enable users to search and filter movies efficiently.
* Provide detailed movie information to assist decision-making.
* Allow users to change cities without creating a new account.
* Maintain a permanent booking history.
* Generate digital tickets immediately after successful booking.

---

# 5. Business Objectives

The platform should:

* Increase online movie ticket sales.
* Improve customer engagement.
* Reduce dependency on physical ticket counters.
* Build customer retention through user accounts.
* Establish a scalable architecture capable of supporting multiple cities and theaters.

## Secondary Audience

# 7. Core Product Principles

## Simplicity

Users should be able to complete a booking with minimal effort.

---

## Speed

Every interaction should feel responsive.

Search, filtering, seat selection, and checkout should provide immediate feedback.

---

## Reliability

Seat availability must always remain consistent.

The platform must prevent duplicate bookings.

---

## Security

User accounts and booking information must be securely protected.

Passwords must never be stored in plain text.

Authentication must be secure.

---

## Scalability

The architecture should support:

* Multiple Tier-1 cities : Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata and Ahmedabad
* Hundreds of theaters
* Hundreds of concurrent users
* Multiple simultaneous bookings
* Future payment gateway integrations

---

# 8. Key User Journey

The ideal booking experience consists of the following stages:

1. Register
2. Login
3. Select City
4. Browse Movies
5. Search or Filter Movies
6. View Movie Details
7. Select Date
8. Select Theater
9. Select Showtime
10. Select Seats
11. Review Booking
12. Confirm Payment
13. Receive Digital Ticket
14. View Booking History

---

# 9. Success Metrics

The product will be considered successful if it achieves:

## User Experience

* Fast page loading
* Smooth booking flow
* Minimal booking abandonment
* High user satisfaction

---

## Functional Success

* Zero duplicate seat bookings
* Successful digital ticket generation
* Accurate booking history
* Reliable city-based filtering

---

## Technical Success

* Responsive across devices
* Secure authentication
* Stable backend APIs
* Efficient database performance
* Consistent seat locking mechanism

---

# 10. Definition of Success

The MVP will be considered complete when a registered user can:

* Register an account.
* Log in securely.
* Select a city.
* Browse movies available in that city.
* View detailed movie information.
* Choose a theater and showtime.
* Select available seats.
* Complete a simulated payment.
* Receive a digital ticket.
* View the booking under "My Bookings."
